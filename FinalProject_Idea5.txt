IDEA#5

~Overview of your idea
A model that can predict a call centre staff's probability of calling in sick on the 10th day, given the percentage of breaks that he spends with his "work buddies".

~What data you will use, and how you will collect it
HR data of a 70-people call centre collected over the last six months containing people's logs of break start and end time, their buddies and a log of their sick days off.

~What the outcome is that you are trying to achieve
A predictive model to forecast the likelihood of a certain call centre staff calling in sick the next day

~Any ideas of modelling techniques it may involve
Community Detection using EigenVectors
